So… why are trees so beautiful?

Because they follow a fractal rule — one simple pattern, repeated over and over — with a pinch of chaos sprinkled on top. The rule makes the pattern; the wildness makes it alive.

That's the beauty of math: from one tiny rule, endless one-of-a-kind trees. Next time you pass a tree, a river or a seashell, you'll see the secret pattern hiding inside. 🌳
